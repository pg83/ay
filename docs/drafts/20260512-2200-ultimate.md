## Cascade pattern blocking L0

`compare_topology.go` computes a Merkle fingerprint per node as
`sha1(kv.p || sorted child fingerprints))` and compares the **multiset** of
fingerprints. Once any leaf's `inputs` or `cmd_args` disagree, its UID is
fine (UIDs are recomputed downstream from kv.p; in our case all 8750 UIDs
already differ from REF because cmd_args content embeds the children too)
but the fingerprint diverges — and every transitive parent inherits the
divergence. **Two leaves dominate**: `util/datetime/parser.rl6.cpp` (Ragel
flag `-CT0` vs `-CG2`) hits 972 ancestors, and the three `runtime_py3`
`.pic.o` nodes (`-DUSE_PYTHON3` mis-placed in the include/flag bundle) hit
539 ancestors each. **Together these four leaves account for ~95% of the
721 fingerprint deficit.**

Three PRs cover all four levels. The remaining seven are housekeeping that

### PR-1 — Ragel flag bundle (closes 972 + 4 cascade, ~95% of L0 gap)

* `util/_/datetime/parser.rl6.cpp`: REF emits `-CG2`, OUR emits `-CT0`.
* `devtools/ymake/lang/makelists/makefile_lang.rl6.cpp`: REF `-lF1`, OUR
  `-CT0`.

OUR codegen hard-codes `-CT0` as the global Ragel default. Upstream sets
flags via `SET(RAGEL_FLAGS ...)` per module (`util/datetime/ya.make` →
`-CG2`; `lang/makelists/ya.make` → `-lF1`). Fix: honour per-module
`RAGEL_FLAGS` overrides in the R6 composer; do **not** silently fall back to
`-CT0`. Drives L3 → 100 for these two and pushes L0 from 91.76 → ~99.6.

### PR-2 — runtime_py3 + ymakeyaml include/flag-bundle order (closes 539×3+3 = ~1620 ancestor-hits)

* `library/python/runtime_py3/{__res,runtime_reg_py3,sitecustomize}.cpp.pic.o`:
  ORDER divergence at pos 90–91. OUR inserts `-DUSE_PYTHON3` **before**
  `-msse2/-msse3`; REF interleaves them. The bundle assembly in CC composer
  emits `-D` macros from one slot, `-mssseX` from another, in the wrong
  relative order for this module.
* `devtools/ymake/libs/ymakeyaml/ymakeyaml{,.reg3}.cpp.o`: ORDER divergence
  at pos 21–22 — `-I python/Include` before `-I re2/include` in REF; OUR
  swaps. Same composer-slot mis-ordering.

Both stem from the same CC argv-slot ordering decision. One PR.

## §4 — L0 plateau (941 unpaired fingerprints)

L0 uses Merkle-style topology fingerprints
(`compare_topology.go:152-165`): `sha1(kv.p || sorted(child fingerprints))`,
i.e. **kv.p of self plus the entire transitive child-fingerprint
distribution**. Any L2/L3 divergence at a leaf re-fingerprints all
paired ancestors that include it via `Deps`.

### Cascade arithmetic

- L2 ∪ L3 divergent paired nodes: **515**.
- Unpaired (15+1): **16**.
- Direct ancestors of these 531 nodes (via ref's child→parent map,
  depth-4 walk) that are AR/LD: **69** (57 AR + 12 LD).
- Of those 69 ancestor ARs/LDs, **43 are themselves L2/L3 paired-divergent**.

So the cascade chain looks like:

```
531 (L2/L3-diff or unpaired leaf node)
  → re-fingerprints every paired ancestor in their .Deps closure
  → ~240 AR/LD paired nodes (3 hops up from a typical leaf)
  → each AR/LD's parent (LD aggregations, top-level targets)
```

A single leaf-CC with one extra/missing `-I` re-hashes:

1. The CC itself (L0 mismatch #1).
2. The owning AR (L0 mismatch #2).
3. Every LD that consumes that AR (L0 mismatch #3 … #N).
4. Every transitive consumer of those LDs (deeper aggregations).

The 8750 - 7809 = **941 fingerprint misses are NOT 941 distinct
"problems" — they are the cascade closure of ~150-200 leaf-level diffs**
(515 L2/L3-divergent paired nodes, of which ~135 are doubly-divergent,
plus 16 unpaired). The Merkle algorithm fans each leaf-level diff out
by a factor of 1.5-2.0 (= average depth of distinct ancestor closure)
across the ~960 AR/LD aggregations in the graph.

### Estimated L0 closure from L2+L3 closure

If PRs L2-1 + L3-1 + L3-2 + L3-3 land (closing ~100 + 153 + 85 + 41 =
~379 leaf-level diffs), the cascade-corrected L0 lift is bounded by:

- **lower bound:** 379 distinct CC/AS/PY/EN fingerprints (their direct
  hashes flip from mismatch → match).
- **upper bound:** ~750-800 fingerprints (the cascade closure: each
  leaf re-fingerprints ~1.5-2.0 ancestors that are currently mismatched
  precisely because of the same leaf).

So after L2-1 + L3-1 + L3-2 + L3-3 → **L0 plausibly jumps 89.25% →
~96.5-97.5%**, mostly from cascade-clean-up of the AR/LD aggregations.

### Residue after L2+L3 PRs

The remaining ~5-50 L0-unpaired fingerprints will be **the §1 unpaired
residue (15 + 1) and their direct AR/LD ancestors**: ~16 + 30 = **~46
fingerprints**, which corresponds to a final L0 ceiling of ~99.5% before
hand-tuning the unpaired residue.
